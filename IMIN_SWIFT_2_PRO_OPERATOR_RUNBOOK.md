## üîß **TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **Problem: "Printer Not Ready" Message**

#### **Quick Fix (90% of cases)**
1. **Tap the Retry button** - system will auto-recover
2. **Wait 3-5 seconds** for initialization
3. **Try printing again**

#### **If Retry Doesn't Work**
1. **Check thermal paper**:
   - Is paper loaded correctly?
   - Is the roll empty?
   - Is paper width 58mm?
2. **Check printer cover**:
   - Is cover closed properly?
   - Any paper jams visible?
3. **Check power**:
   - Is device plugged in?
   - Battery level sufficient?

### **Problem: Poor Print Quality**

#### **Symptoms**: Faded, unclear, or missing text
#### **Solutions**:
1. **Replace thermal paper** - old paper prints poorly
2. **Clean printer head** - use alcohol wipe gently
3. **Check paper alignment** - ensure straight loading
4. **Restart app** if quality doesn't improve

### **Problem: Paper Jam**

#### **Symptoms**: Paper doesn't feed or gets stuck
#### **Solutions**:
1. **Open printer cover** carefully
2. **Remove jammed paper** gently - don't force
3. **Reload paper roll** ensuring correct direction
4. **Close cover** and test print

### **Problem: App Shows "Not an Imin Device"**

#### **Symptoms**: Module test fails, no printing options
#### **Solutions**:
1. **Verify device model** - must be Imin Swift 2 Pro
2. **Restart the app** completely
3. **Contact IT support** - may need app update
